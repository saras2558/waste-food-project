Waste food has been a problem for many years. Many countries, including the U.S. struggle with this problem. Sometimes people waste food on purpose. This causes many issues like waste of food, more space, and more garbage.

There are many different technologies for it and it helps the food waste/industry. My technology is used to make the food stay a little longer. According to the UN FAO, over 40% of fresh produce goes to waste. Many other solutions I have is a peel which adds a little extra peel on produce to slow the rate of water loss and oxidation, the primary causes of spoilage. That extra peel is completely edible, tasteless, and safe to eat. A variety of plant feedstocks can be used to create our formulations, and luckily these ingredients exist in the peels, seeds, and pulp of all fruits and vegetables, in literally every bite of fruit we take. For example, think of these materials as building blocks, restructuring them in a way that allows us to iterate on what nature created, making our solution into a coating that can be applied to produce.
